Trust Whiterock Conservancy’s River House and Dance Barn, beautiful landscape, and historic setting to be the perfect location for your wedding and reception.

The opportunities to create an exceptional event at Whiterock are endless. Our personal, attentive service will be with you every step of the way.

Tours, general inquiries and reservations can be arranged by contacting the Guest Services at (712) 684-2964 Ext. 112 or email us at: guestinfo@whiterockconservancy.org


Information


The Dance Barn is a beautiful and unique location in Coon Rapids, Iowa. The barn is several thousand square feet on three levels (basement, dance floor, and loft).

The River House, Dance Barn, and spacious lawn can easily accommodate up to 400 guests with limited on grounds seating provided. We have 24 picnic tables (seats 144 people) available for outdoor seating. In case of inclement weather or need for additional seating, please consider renting a tent and chairs for very large groups. Covered seating is available for 235 people.

Wedding ceremonies can be arranged in various locations around the yard, in the barn or an alternative setting on Whiterock Conservancy's landscape. Call us for ideas and pricing. Party hosts are welcome to decorate as they wish, though the natural beauty and country motif can stand on its own.

About the River House Dance Barn and grounds:
 
  • Outside the barn is a bonfire ring, a hog roast spit, and picnic shelter
  • A portable trellis is available to use and decorate as you wish
  • Electrical outlets are available inside and outside of the barn
  • Parking is available for over 200 vehicles. A utility vehicle can transport those in need. There is also a convenient loading and unloading zone near the picnic shelter.
  • The spacious lawn is great for children's play
  • Restrooms are handicap accessible but there is a patio door guide-track to cross on the way to the restroom
  • We provide catering services or you may contact your own outside caterer
  • The Dance Barn is open from May - October
  • Public art, lovely garden, and natural prairie provide a scenic backdrop

  
The base rental fee for the River House, Dance Barn, and surrounding grounds are:
 
  • Event Day (10 am-Midnight) $1,000
  • Plus per person $2.50
  • Woodland Carriage House $165/night (2-night min. stay on weekends). Woodland Carriage House rental is required for staging/dressing area.

    Accommodations


    We will work with you on holding rooms for attendees to reserve before the event date. We suggest you help to assign family members different accommodations to make most efficient use of space.

    We can accommodate 36 people overnight. We suggest that when assigning family members to accommodations that you consider putting wedding party members/college friends in Woodland, extended family members in Oakridge, a family with small children in Hollyhock, older family members in the historic Garst Farmhouse, and reserving Roswell’s Suite or Hollyhock Cottage for yourselves.

    Woodland Carriage House* - 2 rooms, 4 beds (3 queen beds and 1 twin bed); 1 bathroom; full kitchen

    Note: Woodland Carriage House rental is required for staging/dressing area.

    Oakridge Farm House*- 5 bedrooms; 10 beds (3 queen beds and 7 twin beds); 2 Bathrooms; full kitchen, spacious living room

    Hollyhock Cottage* - 2 rooms, 4 beds (1 queen beds, a twin bunk bed, and 1 queen size futon); 1 bathroom; full kitchen

    Historic Garst Farmhouse - 2 rooms with private bath, 1 handicap accesssible room with bath, 2 rooms with a shared bath.

    *2-night minimum stay on weekends required for Oakridge, Woodland, and Hollyhock Cottage.

    We also welcome overnight stays in our primitive campgrounds. Tents, campers, RVs, horse trailers are all welcomed! (Generators are not allowed at the Main Campgrounds across from the River House.) The local county RV park provides electrical and water hookups.

    Driving Directions to Whiterock Conservancy Headquarters



    Arriving from Des Moines: Whiterock is about 70 miles from Des Moines; trip takes about 1 1/4 hours. From I-35/I-80 on the NW corner of Des Moines, exit onto Hwy 141 towards Perry. Drive through Perry, Bagley, and Bayard on Hwy 141. Whiterock Conservancy is 6 miles west of Bayard on Hwy 141.

    Arriving from Omaha: Whiterock is about 100 miles from Omaha; trip takes about 2 hours. Take I-80 east to Hwy 71 Atlantic/Audubon exit. Go north on Hwy 71, through Audubon, to Hwy 141. Turn east (right) and drive through Dedham and Coon Rapids. Whiterock is located 1/2 mile east of the convenience store in Coon Rapids on the south side of Hwy 141.

    Arriving from Ames: Whiterock is about 65 miles from Ames; trip takes about 1 1/4 hours. From I-35 in Ames, go west on Hwy 30. Turn south (left) onto Hwy 25 at Scranton. Turn west (right) on Hwy 141 at Bayard. Whiterock is 6 miles west of Bayard, on Hwy 141.
