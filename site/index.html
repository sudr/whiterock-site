Whiterock Conservancy


We are an Iowa non-profit, 501(c)(3), land trust dedicated to conserving and protecting Iowa's natural resources and engaging the public with the environment through outdoor recreation and education.

Your Iowa Vacation Starts Here


Now is a great time to schedule a weekend, a whole week, or simply an afternoon trip to Whiterock Conservancy.

We offer a variety of lodging options great for gathering with family, a couples get away or just some quiet time for yourself. Spend a night in the comfortable, individual rooms in the historic Garst Farmhouse, the home of Roswell and Elizabeth Garst. Or choose to rent one of our country cottages with comfortable beds and full kitchens. We also have campground options, ranging from remote, primitive areas to RV and camper-accessible.

Tours, general inquiries and reservations can be arranged by contacting the Guest Services at (712) 684-2964 Ext. 112 or email us at: guestinfo@whiterockconservancy.org

See all the accommodation options and make a reservation online.

 


New Visitor's Center and Event Space for Rent



Renovations are almost complete on the Bur Oak Visitor's Center. The former meeting space has been converted to Whiterock Conservancy offices and a new reception desk that can accommodate guests checking in, renting equipment and learning more about the conservancy. The open air covered picnic area has been enclosed with large windows, temperature and wind control, and a soon to be inviting wood burning stove. The space can seat 60 and is perfect for small reception, meetings, and office retreats surrounded by the beautiful views. 

 General inquiries and reservations can be arranged by contacting Guest Services at (712) 684-2964 Ext. 112 or email us at: guestinfo@whiterockconservancy.org

Driving Directions to Whiterock Conservancy Headquarters


Arriving from Des Moines: Whiterock is about 70 miles from Des Moines; trip takes about 1 1/4 hours. From I-35/I-80 on the NW corner of Des Moines, exit onto Hwy 141 towards Perry. Drive through Perry, Bagley, and Bayard on Hwy 141. Whiterock Conservancy is 6 miles west of Bayard on Hwy 141.


Arriving from Omaha: Whiterock is about 100 miles from Omaha; trip takes about 2 hours. Take I-80 east to Hwy 71 Atlantic/Audubon exit. Go north on Hwy 71, through Audubon, to Hwy 141. Turn east (right) and drive through Dedham and Coon Rapids. Whiterock is located 1/2 mile east of the convenience store in Coon Rapids on the south side of Hwy 141.


Arriving from Ames: Whiterock is about 65 miles from Ames; trip takes about 1 1/4 hours. From I-35 in Ames, go west on Hwy 30. Turn south (left) onto Hwy 25 at Scranton. Turn west (right) on Hwy 141 at Bayard. Whiterock is 6 miles west of Bayard, on Hwy 141.
