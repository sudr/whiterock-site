Host Your Reunion With Us!


We offer a relaxing and fun filled getaway in the scenic Middle Raccoon River Valley.

Whiterock Conservancy's Guest Services can help you select lodging and activities for your reunion. We can accommodate overnight groups of about 36 people, with room for more participants for a variety of day time activities.



Family, Classmates, and Co-Worker Reunions

Oakridge Farm House makes an ideal home base for reunions. The Oakridge kitchen is equipped to handle up to 30 people. The yard is lovely and private. A nearby garage provides shelter if it is raining. Oak Ridge offers full amenities, including A/C, TV/VCR, antennae TV, iron and ironing board, basketball, croquet set, dart board, horseshoes, an extensive library and all linens. The facility is equipped with a fire ring and firewood and is near hiking trails and fishing ponds.

The Oakridge House officially sleeps 13 people (5 bedrooms, 3 queens, 7 twins). Extra children can be tucked into the house, though at about 18 people, the two bathrooms become a constraint.





For extra sleeping quarters, consider Woodland Carriage House, which is about 1 mile from Oakridge. Woodland can sleep 7, and has room for a few extras. Our Main Campground is adjacent to Woodland for those who want to experience our camping option.





The Hollyhock Cottage and Historic Garst Farmhouse combination can sleep a total of 17. These facilities are less than 5 miles from Oakridge. The individual rooms in our farmhouse is our most elegant lodging. We do have some constraints: the farmhouse kitchen access is limited by guests (but subject to discussion); there is no room to tuck in extra people; and of course it is a relatively more expensive choice. If you want wonderful accommodations and pampering (i.e. you can arrange for us to do the cooking plus we do all cleaning); this combination makes a good choice.


Visit our lodging web page for more details about all of our overnight options.
